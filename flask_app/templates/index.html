Parking tickets...

They can ruin your day and hurt your wallet. As a group, we set out to learn more about parking tickets. What is the most common type of parking violation? Where are they more likely to occur? How often are they issued? Could weather conditions affect ticket issuance behaviors?

To answer the above questions, we retrieved the historic Philadelphia parking violations data from 2017 (OpenDataPhilly) and the historical weather data from that same year (OpenWeather API). We then generated a series of visualizations and an interactive multi-layered map to take a closer look at the data.

P.S. For considerations including ease of work, Github repo storage, and most importantly the responsiveness and performance while loading/generating the visuals, we decided to take a random sample of only 30% of all the parking violations data in 2017. The violation count per month was consistent throughout the year thus we believed there will be minimal negative impact on our visulizations.
